About Us

G3 Design and Drafting Solutions is a leading provider of innovative design and drafting services. We combine expertise, creativity, and cutting-edge technology to deliver exceptional results for our clients worldwide.

Our Services

Design Services

1. Conceptual Design:
• Initial design concepts and sketches for projects. • Feasibility studies and preliminary design proposals. 2. Detailed Design:
• Full-scale, detailed design plans for electrical systems, architectural layouts, and other engineering projects. • Design modifications and optimizations based on client feedback.
3. System Design:
• Electrical system designs, including power distribution and control systems. • Integration of PLC/SCADA systems and other advanced technologies.
4. 3D Modeling and Visualization:
• Creating 3D models of design concepts for better visualization. • Virtual walkthroughs and simulations to help clients understand the final product.

Drafting Services


1. Technical Drafting:
• Creation of technical drawings for construction, manufacturing, and assembly. • Drafting of electrical schematics, wiring diagrams, and layout plans.
2. As-Built Drawings:
• Accurate representation of completed projects reflecting changes made during construction. • Documentation for maintenance and future modifications.
3. Compliance and Standards:
• Ensuring all drawings meet industry standards and regulatory requirements. • Preparing documentation for permits and approvals.
4. Coordination and Integration:
• Coordinating with other engineering disciplines (MEP, civil, structural) to ensure design integration. • Managing design changes and updates across different project phases.

Consulting Services


1. Design Review and Analysis:
• Reviewing existing designs for improvements and compliance. • Conducting value engineering and cost analysis.
2. Project Management Support:
• Providing drafting and design support for project management tasks. • Assisting with project planning and execution strategies.
3. Technical Advisory:
• Offering expert advice on design and drafting best practices. • Recommending technological solutions and innovations.

Additional Solution


1. Training and Support:
• Providing training for clients on using design and drafting software. • Offering ongoing support and troubleshooting for design-related issues.
2. Documentation and Reporting:
• Preparing detailed reports and documentation related to design and drafting projects. • Creating user manuals and operational guides for completed systems.
